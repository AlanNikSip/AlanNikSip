INNA: YO

Yo (english: I or Me) is the sixth studio album by Romanian singer Inna, released on 31 May 2019 by Global Records and Roc Nation. Inna began work on the record in 2016; inspired by a recent trip to Latin America, she decided the album would feature only Spanish language material. Inna contributed significantly to the songwriting process of Yo, and collaborated extensively with Romanian producer David Ciente on its songs. An experimental and heavily gypsy music-influenced effort, Yo marks the first time Inna took control over an album's creative process; it acts as a departure from her past EDM releases. Lyrically, Inna sings about love, and presents several female characters seen from different perspectives throughout the album. I was in the studio, [...] listening to some instrumentals [...], I started with some lines and then, it turned out really easy to write the lyrics in Spanish. - said Inna.

Inna began working on Yo in 2016 and around 50 songs were composed from which to select the album's final tracklist. After her first official tour in Canada and the United States in September–October 2018, Inna uploaded a short film on her YouTube channel on 16 October (her birthday) titled Yo.
